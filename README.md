## Philosophy

phantom_ follows one simple principle:

> **Your player. Your sources. Your data.**

Your playlists, credentials and settings stay on your device.

There are intentionally:

- 🚫 No accounts
- 🚫 No cloud synchronization
- 🚫 No advertising
- 🚫 No telemetry

---

## Features

- 📺 Xtream Codes & M3U
- ⭐ Favorites & Groups
- 📅 EPG
- 📦 Catch-up / Archive
- 🔒 Local AES encryption
- 🔑 Optional PIN protection
- 🛰 Optional Relay support
- 🛡 Relay Kill Switch
- 🌍 Optional VPN verification
- ⏱ Sleep Timer
- 📌 Program reminders
